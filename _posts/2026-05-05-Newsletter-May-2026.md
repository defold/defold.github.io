## Defold 1.12.4 Has Been Released

![Defold 1.12.4 Has Been Released](/images/posts/newsletter-may-2026/defold_1124.webp)

Highlights:

- Free Camera mode in Scene View
- glTF validator integrated into the asset pipeline
- New shader precision options
- 16 texture units per draw for better rendering capabilities
- Text rendering performance boost
- Smaller HTML5/WASM builds, with an empty project now 1.06 MB
- Editor, rendering, and runtime performance improvements

We have also added new examples, refreshed Defold's website, and updated the Rive extension.

[Updated Rive Extension](https://forum.defold.com/t/updated-rive-extension/82642)

[Check Release Notes](https://defold.com/2026/05/04/Defold-1-12-4/)
