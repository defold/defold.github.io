Why join the server?

Discord works best when you want short feedback loops, quick back-and-forth, and a more conversational space around your work.

Ask quick questions

Get unstuck on small issues, sanity-check an approach, or ask where to look next before turning it into a deeper investigation.

Show work in progress

Share screenshots, clips, and prototypes to get early reactions, suggestions, and practical feedback from other developers.

Meet collaborators

Find people to test your game, discuss ideas with, or connect with artists, programmers, and other creators in the Defold scene.

Stay close to the community

Follow day-to-day discussion, see what others are building, and stay near the flow of activity around Defold between larger announcements.

Use the right channel for the job

Discord is great for real-time conversation, but some topics are better handled in the forum or through the broader support pages.
