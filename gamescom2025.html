The Defold team is in Cologne for Gamescom!

Reasons to meet

We’re excited to connect with game studios and indie developers to show how Defold can be the perfect engine for your next project — lightweight, powerful, and production-ready.

We’re also looking to meet with publishers to demonstrate why Defold is a trusted, fully featured engine with all the essential services you need to successfully publish games built with Defold.

Finally, we’d love to talk with game technology and service providers about how we can integrate your products and services into the Defold ecosystem, bringing even more value to our developer community.

Are you a publisher?

At Defold, we understand what publishers need: proven technology, smooth integrations, and dependable support. Whether you’re working with indie teams or large studios, Defold gives you the confidence and flexibility to deliver high-quality games — on time and on every major platform.

Production ready

Defold is battle-tested and production-ready, powering games with millions of installs and more than half a billion plays in the past year alone.

Trusted technology

Defold partners with industry leaders like MoonActive, Poki, and Rive. We’re approved for PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox, recommended by Google, and trusted by studios and indies worldwide.

Essential services

We know how important it is to easily connect analytics, app economy, and game services to your games. That’s why Defold offers a wide range of battle-tested integrations, ready to use out of the box.

Support

We help with any technical issues or questions during and after publishing. With our support, you can be confident the services you depend on are fully integrated and kept up to date, letting you focus on launching and running your games.

Are you a developer?

Defold gives you a rock-solid foundation to build, polish, and ship games across platforms. From stable, predictable updates to powerful scripting and artist-friendly tools, Defold combines performance, flexibility, and ease of use — so you can focus on creating great gameplay, not fighting your engine.

Stable releases

With a four-week release cycle and two-week beta period, Defold delivers updates you can rely on. Breaking changes are exceptionally rare, and crashes are few and far between.

Artist friendly

Assemble and layout your game in our scene editor. Build levels, player characters, bosses, screens and user interface components all from within our tools.

Fully scriptable

Build game logic in Lua, customize the rendering pipeline, and extend the editor to fit your workflow. For deeper integration, use our C++/C# SDK to add native functionality and introduce new resource types.

Performance and stability

Defold is engineered to minimize memory use, disk space, and CPU load. Its small build size makes it ideal for web and mobile games — just 1.1 MB for web, 1.97 MB for Android, and 1.47 MB for iOS.

We make no claim that Defold is the best game engine out there for any game, but we dare to say that Defold is the best game engine for some games.
