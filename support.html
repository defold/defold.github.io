Professional support

Additionally, at Defold we offer expert guidance, resources, and assistance to help ensure the long-term success of your game.Choose the collaboration path that matches your needs.

Game developers and studios

We help developers and studios of all sizes make games that are lean, performant, stable, and easier to ship.

  • Optimization of game performance and stability
  • New editor, engine, and tooling features tailored to your needs
  • Technical assistance during development and production
  • Porting to additional platforms or updating existing platform support

With Defold support, your team can focus on gameplay and production while we help on the technical side.

Vendors and platforms

We help services and platforms integrate cleanly with Defold and stay maintained as the engine and ecosystem evolve.

  • Guidance on integrating your platform or product with Defold
  • Engine and editor integrations for your service
  • Ongoing maintenance and updates for those integrations
  • Middleware design and implementation support for game engine workflows

This helps your product fit naturally into the Defold toolchain and stay reliable for developers.

Publisher and distributors

We help publishers keep analytics, app economy, and service integrations reliable across portfolio launches and updates.

  • Battle-tested service integrations ready for Defold games
  • Additional integrations built around your publishing needs
  • Compliance updates for evolving iOS and Android platform requirements
  • Technical assistance during and after the publishing process

This lets your team focus on launch, operations, and portfolio management while the technical foundations stay current.
