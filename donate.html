Defold is and always will be free. We offer free cloud builds as well, and we cover its infrastructure costs from the donations. The Defold Foundation is dedicated to the engine development, maintenance, and support, and funds it through community donations, corporate partnerships, partnership deals, and support contracts.

Your support helps keep Defold sustainable, actively developed, and free to use for everyone.

Why support Defold?

As a software foundation, we rely on community donations and corporate partnerships to make long-term Defold development sustainable.

To pay developers

Funding helps pay developers working directly on the engine, editor, documentation, and surrounding tooling.

To run core services

Support covers hosting and infrastructure used by build services, websites, forums, and other shared community systems.

To buy test hardware

Funding helps purchase hardware and devices needed for platform testing, debugging, and engine development.

To attend events and promote Defold

Support helps cover travel and event costs when the Defold team visits conferences and meets developers in person.

Choose a support option

Support Defold with recurring or one-time community funding, or start a corporate partnership if your team wants a deeper collaboration path.
