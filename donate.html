Why donate?

As a software foundation we rely on community donations and corporate partnerships to ensure the long term sustainability of the project. The funds received from donations and partnerships are used for:

  • Pay developers working on Defold
  • Pay for hosting of services
  • Purchase of hardware for testing and development
  • Pay for travel expenses when visiting conferences
