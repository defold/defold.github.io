Why partner with Defold?

Corporate partnerships help fund engine work directly while giving your organization dedicated collaboration time and stronger visibility in the Defold ecosystem.

Dedicated support time

Use partnership hours on specific engine, editor, workflow, or ecosystem functionality important to your team.

Prominent visibility

Partners receive mentions on the website, in editor surfaces, blog posts, newsletters, and broader Defold marketing material.

Event access

Corporate partners are invited to participate in special events hosted by the Defold Foundation, in priority order by level.

Long-term support

Partnership funding helps keep Defold sustainable through developer salaries, hosting, test hardware, and ongoing product work.
