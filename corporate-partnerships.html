Corporate partnerships

Our Corporate partnership program lets corporations and organizations fund development of specific functionality in or around the Defold product. Members of the Corporate partnership program will receive dedicated development time from the Defold Team to use on specific projects or features of your choice.

In addition to the dedicated development time, corporate partners will get prominent mentions on the Defold site, in blog posts and newsletters and in the Defold editor. Corporate partners will also get invitations to participate at special events hosted by the foundation.

We offer the following levels of corporate partnership:

Platinum

$48000/year ($4000/month)
  • Your company logo and link in the Platinum corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • Your company logo on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited first to participate in special events
  • 480 hours of development time

Gold

$24000/year ($2000/month)
  • Your company logo and link in the Gold corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • Your company logo on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Platinum partners to participate in special events
  • 240 hours of development time

Silver

$12000/year ($1000/month)
  • Your company logo and link in the Silver corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Gold partners to participate in special events
  • 120 hours of development time

Bronze

$6000/year ($500/month)
  • Your company logo and link in the Bronze corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Silver partners to participate in special events
  • 60 hours of development time

If you are unable to find a suitable corporate partnership level you can reach out to us at partner@defold.se to discuss a custom partnership level.
